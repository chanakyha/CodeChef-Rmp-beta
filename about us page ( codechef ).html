ABOUT

CODECHEF :


CodeChef is a competitive programming community of programmers from across the globe .. It was created as a platform to help programmers make it big in the world of algorithms , computer programming and programming contests. Codechef promotes constant learning and friendly programming competitions for its users , along with the support of its large community of problem curators . Codechef also partners with schools , colleges and student groups to create local codechef chapters , meet-ups orientation sessions and programming workshops . In addition , Codechef sponsors coding requests and teams of Indian programmers as part of its GO FOR GOLD INITIATIVE for ICPC and IOI .
